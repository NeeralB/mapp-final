Welcome to MUNICIPAL

Stay updated with your local politics

MUNICIPAL is an application that keeps you up to date with the local politics in your area. MUNICIPAL aims to do 4 things: find the latest statements/accusations said by local politicians, find the latest bills passed by your local politicians, and identifies all politicians representing your area as well as the closest local polling centers.
